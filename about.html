About HealthInsight

HealthInsight is a web-based health risk assessment platform designed to help users evaluate their health status by entering personal information and selecting symptoms.

The system calculates Body Mass Index (BMI), analyzes symptoms, and provides possible health conditions along with probability and risk levels.

This project was developed as a final year Computer Science project to demonstrate the use of frontend web technologies in solving healthcare challenges.
