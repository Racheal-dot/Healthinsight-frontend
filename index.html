About HealthInsight

HealthInsight is a web-based health risk assessment platform designed to help users better understand potential health conditions based on personal health data and symptom. By entering basic information such as age, height, body measurements, and symptoms, the system analyzes the information using a medical diagnosis API and provides possible health conditions along with useful health insights. This tool is designed for educational and informational purposes and should not replace professional medical consultation.

How the Health Assessment Works

Enter Your Health Information

Provide basic information such as age, height, weight, blood pressure, and other relevant health details.

Select Your Symptoms

Choose the symptoms you are currently experiencing from the list provided.

Health Risk Analysis

The system analyzes the information using a medical diagnosis API to identify possible health conditions.

View Health Insights

Receive possible health conditions, symptom explanations, and recommended next steps.
